Our Story

Laura

Kyle and I met more than four years ago, despite the improbalities of finding each other, we did it, and we are very lucky for it. When I met him I didn´t really think that he was my person, that who was meant to be with me for the rest of my life, but I discovered an intelligent, strong, supportive and funny man in him, who has been with me when things had gotten difficult. He has encouraged me to try doing new stuff and showed me how fun winter can be. I love exploring the world and life with him. We have lived very close (mini van close) but also very far to each other, both experiences had helped us understand and love each other better, fighting each day to be a great team. Now that we decided to join our lives we want to celebrate with you!

Kyle

During the first few months of Peace Corps, I was invited by a fellow volunteer to a place called Casa Semillas (house of seeds) to learn about sustainability and organic growing. Being that I was an environmental education volunteer, I thought it would be a great opportunity. A shy girl named Laura was one of the owners and teachers there. After a few lessons, I started volunteering at the organization. Eventually, we would all celebrate after hard days, or go out in the city when we had time. I started peeling away at Laura's shy exterior and found a goofy, compassionate, honest person with a great heart. After a while we would spend more time together apart from the group, having our own jokes, finding deeper conversations and developing a solid connection. One night, she stole a kiss from me. We started going out, and she started help me with projects at my Peace Corps site in Pabellon de Arteaga, Aguascalientes. Later I invited her to an excursion to a beautiful place called the Huasteca, a paradise oasis, where we realized how similar we are. All her happiness and positive vibes trapped me. We have had many adventures and she never complains when it gets difficult or uncomfortable. Her patience and positivity has helped me a lot and I am excited to know we will have a life full of great things. She is my bedrock, my closest friend, and I love her with all my soul. I hope you can make it to our party and have fun with us!
